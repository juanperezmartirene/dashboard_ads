Video Overview

The visualizations on the Overview Tab showcase ads run by 4 pages between 06/01/2020 and 11/22/2020, where the ads have been tagged for a message type of Any, a topic of Any, and a message tone of Civil or Uncivil. The results have additionally been filtered to audiences in 51 states who are Female, Male, or Unknown and ages: 7 categories. If the refresh button to the upper left is displayed, the visualizations below no longer match the filter criteria on the left.

Note: All spending related to an ad buy is credited to the Monday of the week the ad buy started.
